What are QuickNotes?

For a few weeks I worked on a project called QuickNotes. They're essentially the same as Windows 7's sticky notes, where you could open up a little window on your desktop and type in it to give yourself reminders, or take notes in.

However, I wanted to differentiate my program from sticky notes a bit, so I decided to add the option to save a note that you find particularly important or long, for added convenience.

Click the save button on a note...

And it'll be there whenever you press the saved note button!

(Unfortunately the save button only saves the text in your most recently created note. Coding is hard.)
